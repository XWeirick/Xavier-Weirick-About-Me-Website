About Me

My Favorite Games Currently

As I said, I like to do some PC and Console gaming every now and then when I am not playing the guitar or hanging out. I have always liked shooting games like Call of Duty (I’ve been putting a lot of time into the newest one!), and more recently, I have gotten into more chill games like Stardew Valley. One of my favorite games right now though, is MLB The Show 24. I have really enjoyed playing the whole series, going back to MLB The Show 15 when I was 11 years old. I have always had fun creating my own character and absolutely dominating in the Major Leagues.

On the other hand, my two other favorite games right now are story-based games that could possibly go down as part of the top 10 in modern story driven games. These two games are Cyberpunk 2077 and Black Myth: Wukong. Cyberpunk 2077 takes place in a futuristic, cyberpunk themed city of Night City that is driven by Japanese culture, and it lies between Los Angeles and San Francisco. The main story is about fighting back to gain freedom against the mega corporations that have had control over the lives of the people of Night City.

Black Myth: Wukong, takes place in 7th Century China, and is heavily inspired off the novel Journey to the West. You play as a monkey labeled as “The Destined One”, as you go through the world in 6 different chapters to find the 6 different relics that Sun Wukong himself had split into after his fight against Erlang Shen. Some of My Favorite Music Genres to Play on the Guitar

As I also said, I like to play and practice the electric guitar when I am not playing video games. I have only been playing for about a year (at the time of writing this), but I have found myself dedicating a lot of time to the instrument, because I really think that I actually enjoy playing this instrument, and I see myself sticking to playing it for most of my life. The genres that I like to play on the guitar are also genres that I love listening to. The first genre that I like playing is Metal. Mostly, I like to play older songs by Bring Me The Horizon, Slipknot, and some Suicide Silence. What I really like about Metal is the ability to be able to make heavy music and varying paces, such as slowing it way down for a brutal breakdown or speeding it way up for a fast chorus.

Another genre that I really enjoy playing is Pop Punk. I like to mostly play songs by Green Day or Blink-182. Although I like playing the genre, I do not spend a lot of time playing it, so I know very little. What I like about playing the genre, however, is the simplicity of playing it. Most of the song structure in Pop Punk songs are mostly built around power chords (wiki link https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Power_chord ), which makes playing the songs really simple while still being able to provide a full sound and melody through the amp.

The last genre that I like to play is a mix of Alternative Rock and Metal. I play almost exclusively songs by Deftones, as I have not really delved into the genre, but they are also in my top 3 favorite bands. What I appreciate the most of this genre is being able to mix rock and metal elements with slow and ambient sounds to create a rollercoaster of a song, which I have found to really enjoy in some of the music that I listen to.
