I decided to make a page dedicated to highlighting some of my favorite bands, because listening to music is one of my favorite hobbies. I also think that music is a great way to express emotion, whether it is by the lyrics, or the instruments. I believe that there is a certain type of music for everyone out there, and they might not even know they like it! I also hope that by going through my list of bands, you give some of them a listen! However, music taste is subjective, so any of these bands might be your cup of tea!
